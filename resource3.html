Who are the Food Runners?

The Food Runners are a community of people who take in donations for food and use it to help allivate hunger in San Francisco. They currently deliver 17 tons of food each week that would otherwise be thrown away. Their address is 2579 Washington St, San Francisco, CA 94115, and you can call them at (415) 282-1900. Instead of food going to waste, you can donate to them here.
