What does this place offer?

Kezar Stadium had opened up during this pandemic as a food bank. There'll be volunteers there to manage the lines, assist with the check-in, and package the food. You would need to wear a face mask, and closed toed shoes. Please be aware that this food bank is outside, and due to the weather, you might need to bring excessive items with you. The food bank is open from 12pm-4pm, and onsite registration is required. For more information click here.
